About

Pomodoro 50 is designed to help you manage your time using the Pomodoro Technique. This technique involves breaking your work into intervals, separated by short breaks.

This page will help you understand how to use the Pomodoro 50 app, explain the features available.

Love this app? How about buying me a coffee to support the development of this app. Thank you!

How to use?

This is the main page of the Pomodoro 50 app. Here you can start the timer, pause it, and reset it. Whenever you intentionally stop the timer, the app will prompt you to enter a reason for stopping. This is to help us understand why you stopped the timer and give you insights on your productivity.

Insights page will show you a summary of your productivity. It will show you the number of Pomodoros you have completed, the number of hours you have worked, and the number of hours you have taken breaks. This will help you understand how you are spending your time and how you can further improve your productivity.

The History page will show you a list of all the Pomodoros you have completed. It will show you the date and time you started the Pomodoro, the duration of the Pomodoro, and the reason you stopped the Pomodoro. This will help you understand how you are spending your time and how you can improve your productivity.

The Settings page will allow you to customize the app to your liking. You can change the duration of the Pomodoro, the duration of the short break, and the duration of the long break. You can also change the theme of the app to light or dark mode.

The Profile page will allow you to update your email, and password. You can also delete your account if you no longer wish to use the app. This page will help you manage your account and keep your information up to date. Also, you can see your login streak here.
